Schematic Design Of Transistor Level XOR & XNOR Gate.

Having gained sufficient knowledge about series and parallel connection, we now move towards designing transistor level schematic for any given combinational logic. This is done by analyzing the kmap of the given combination for p- and n-switches and then deducing the required series or parallel combination.The following example will give you an idea about how to go for designing a combinational logic using transistors.we want to design transistor level schematic of (AB+CD)
